# 🧠 InsureGenie

### A Product by **Team DataNex**

**InsureGenie** is a next-generation digital insurance platform designed to transform insurance from a passive financial product into a proactive wellness partner. By leveraging real-time data and machine learning, the platform provides instant risk assessment and gamifies healthy living to lower premiums for users and reduce risk for insurers.

---

## 🚀 Project Vision

Traditional insurance relies on historical data and manual underwriting processes that can take weeks. **InsureGenie** disrupts this model by introducing **Reverse Underwriting**—calculating risk profiles in real-time based on active lifestyle data.

This approach shifts the industry focus from simply **"paying for accidents"** to actively **"investing in health."**

---

## 💡 Key Features

### 1️⃣ Reverse Underwriting Engine

* Uses specialized Machine Learning models to analyze:

  * Health metrics (BMI, heart rate)
  * Driving history
  * Lifestyle habits
* Generates an **instant risk label**:

  * 🟢 Low Risk
  * 🟡 Medium Risk
  * 🔴 High Risk

---

### 2️⃣ Dynamic Risk Breakdown

Provides users with a transparent view of their risk profile, weighted across three core pillars:

| Pillar       | Weight |
| ------------ | ------ |
| 🏥 Health    | 50%    |
| 🌿 Lifestyle | 30%    |
| 🚗 Driving   | 20%    |

This transparency helps users clearly understand **what affects their insurance risk** and how to improve it.

---

### 3️⃣ Wellness Dashboard

A centralized hub where users can:

* Monitor their **Total Score**
* Track their current **Reward Badge**:

  * 🥉 Bronze
  * 🥈 Silver
  * 🥇 Gold
* View their latest **activity logs**
* Understand their real-time insurance standing

---

### 4️⃣ Gamified Rewards System

Encourages healthy behavior by converting physical activity into points:

* **1 point per 100 steps** 👣
* **1 point per 50 calories burned** 🔥
* **1 point per 10 active minutes** ⏱️

This system makes insurance **interactive, motivating, and rewarding**.

---

### 5️⃣ Real-Time Activity Tracking

Automatically records:

* Steps
* Calories burned
* Active minutes
* Distance traveled

Users receive **immediate feedback** on how their lifestyle impacts their insurance profile.

---

### 6️⃣ Intelligent Authentication

Provides secure and frictionless onboarding using:

* **Google Authentication Integration**
* Personalized user profiles
* Secure session management

---

### Value for Insurers
